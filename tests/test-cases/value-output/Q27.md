# value-Q27: What is the average delivered net revenue per customer with at least one delivered order?

## Question

What is the average delivered net revenue per customer with at least one delivered order?

## Reference SQL

```sql
WITH delivered_customer_revenue AS (SELECT o.customer_id, SUM(oi.quantity * oi.unit_price - oi.discount_amount) AS delivered_net_revenue FROM orders o JOIN order_items oi USING (order_id) WHERE o.order_status = 'delivered' GROUP BY 1) SELECT ROUND(AVG(delivered_net_revenue), 2) AS avg_delivered_revenue_per_active_customer FROM delivered_customer_revenue
```

## Explanation

Group by customer_id to calculate the total net revenue of their 'delivered' orders. Then, calculate the average of this total revenue across all active customers.
