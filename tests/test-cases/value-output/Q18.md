## Question

What percentage of total net revenue comes from the top 10% of customers by lifetime net revenue?

## Reference SQL

```sql
WITH customer_revenue AS (SELECT o.customer_id, SUM(oi.quantity * oi.unit_price - oi.discount_amount) AS lifetime_net_revenue FROM orders o JOIN order_items oi USING (order_id) GROUP BY 1), ranked AS (SELECT customer_id, lifetime_net_revenue, ROW_NUMBER() OVER (ORDER BY lifetime_net_revenue DESC, customer_id) AS rn, COUNT(*) OVER () AS total_customers FROM customer_revenue) SELECT ROUND(100.0 * SUM(CASE WHEN rn <= CEIL(total_customers * 0.1) THEN lifetime_net_revenue ELSE 0 END) / SUM(lifetime_net_revenue), 4) AS top_decile_revenue_share_pct FROM ranked
```

## Explanation

Calculate lifetime net revenue per customer and rank them descending. Count the total customers. Sum the revenue of the top 10% (rank <= 0.1 * total_customers) and divide it by the total overall revenue.
