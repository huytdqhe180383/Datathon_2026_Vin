# value-Q30: Among customers with at least 5 orders, what is the median lifetime net revenue?

## Question

Among customers with at least 5 orders, what is the median lifetime net revenue?

## Reference SQL

```sql
WITH customer_revenue AS (SELECT o.customer_id, COUNT(DISTINCT o.order_id) AS order_count, SUM(oi.quantity * oi.unit_price - oi.discount_amount) AS lifetime_net_revenue FROM orders o JOIN order_items oi USING (order_id) GROUP BY 1) SELECT ROUND(median(lifetime_net_revenue), 2) AS median_lifetime_net_revenue_5plus_orders FROM customer_revenue WHERE order_count >= 5
```

## Explanation

Group by customer_id to count distinct orders and sum lifetime net revenue. Filter for customers with at least 5 orders, then find the median of their lifetime net revenues.
